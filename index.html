ReactionBot

During my time at UW HCDE, we built a Slack chatbot in Node.js and Swift that captures
user facial expression and automatically attaches emoji to text messages.


We researched the implications of this system in text-based communication and submitted the research paper
"ReactionBot: Exploring the Effects of Expression-Triggered Emoji in Text Messages" to CSCW 2018.
